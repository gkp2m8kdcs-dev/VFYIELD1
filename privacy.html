PRIVACY POLICY

Your data stays on this device

VFYIELD is built around a single principle: nothing you analyze leaves your iPhone. The image and text engines run entirely on-device, request only the permissions they need, and never contact a server.

Last updated: July 2026

Summary in one paragraph

VFYIELD collects no data about you. It performs no analytics, sends no network requests, embeds no third-party SDKs, and does not track you across apps or websites. The app uses only two operating-system permissions — Camera and Photos — and only when you explicitly tap the corresponding button. Nothing you analyze is transmitted, sold, or stored outside your device.

1. What we collect

PERSONAL DATA
None. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifier.
USAGE DATA
None. We do not record which features you use, how long you use them, or how you arrived at any screen.
DIAGNOSTICS
None. No crash reporting, no performance telemetry, no A/B testing infrastructure is bundled into the app.
LOCATION
Never accessed. VFYIELD does not request your location.
CONTACTS
Never accessed. VFYIELD does not read your contacts.
TRACKING
Never. VFYIELD does not use the IDFA, does not fingerprint your device, and contains no advertising SDKs.

2. What we store on your device

VFYIELD writes a small amount of data into the app's private sandbox so that your past analyses survive when you close and reopen the app:

  • History of analyses — For each run, VFYIELD stores a thumbnail of the captured or imported image (downsized to a maximum edge of 512 pixels), the dominant hue, the verdict, the score breakdown, and (for text comparisons) the original left/right inputs you typed. This data lives in the app's Documents directory, which is private to the app and inaccessible to other apps.
  • Two preference toggles — Whether haptic feedback is enabled, and whether monospaced metrics are enabled. Each is a single boolean stored under pref.haptics and pref.mono in UserDefaults.
  • Selected accent theme — A single string (copper, iris, or mint) stored under pref.theme.

You can erase all of this at any time: open the app, go to Library → History, and tap Clear. Removing the app uninstalls everything stored by VFYIELD from your device.

3. Permissions we ask for, and why

CAMERA
Used only when you tap Capture with Camera. The camera is not accessed in the background and no image is uploaded, even if you are online.
PHOTOS (READ)
Used only when you tap Choose from Library on the image analysis screen. We use the modern PHPickerViewController, which runs in a separate process and does not require granting your entire photo library to the app.
PHOTOS (WRITE)
Used only when you tap Save to Photos in the share sheet. VFYIELD saves a generated composite image (the rendered result card) and nothing else.

You can revoke any of these at any time from iOS Settings → VFYIELD → Privacy.

4. Network behavior

VFYIELD does not initiate network requests. The app binary contains no analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, no crash reporter, and no remote-configuration service. During normal use the app operates entirely offline. We may update the app over the App Store, but updates are delivered through Apple and are not initiated by the app itself.

5. Children

VFYIELD is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 (or the age defined by local regulation). Because the app collects no information at all, this requirement is met by construction.

6. Your rights

Because VFYIELD collects no data, there is no account to delete, no data to export, and no profile to request. If you want the app to forget everything, delete it — that is sufficient.

For questions about this policy, contact Butraminamhegqulad@gmail.com.

7. Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy — for example, if a future version of VFYIELD adds an opt-in feature that contacts a server — we will update the policy on this page and surface the change inside the app via the Privacy & Notices screen. The change date at the top of this document will reflect the most recent update.

8. Disclaimer

VFYIELD is an educational similarity tool. The patterns it surfaces are heuristic comparisons for learning and curiosity — not scientific measurements, medical advice, financial guidance, or expert analysis. Do not rely on its output for safety-, health-, finance-, or legal-critical decisions.
