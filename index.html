HOW IT WORKS

Heuristic, not magic

VFYIELD is an educational instrument. The patterns it surfaces are heuristic comparisons for learning and curiosity — not scientific measurements, medical advice, financial guidance, or expert analysis.

"If you point a camera at a basalt column and compare it to a honeycomb, the app will light up. That reaction is real — the geometry really is shared. What the app does not do is diagnose, decide, or replace expert judgment."

The image engine uses average color, downscale histograms, and structural entropy. The text engine uses unigram cosine, Jaccard overlap, edit distance, and bigram order similarity. Both are deterministic and run without internet access.
