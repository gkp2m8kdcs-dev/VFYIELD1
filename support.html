Frequently asked questions

What does VFYIELD do, exactly?
It compares two things — an image and an archetype, or two text snippets — and reports how much structural kinship they share. The patterns are heuristic: useful for learning, not for deciding. See the overview for the long version.
Does VFYIELD use AI or large language models?
No. The image engine uses average color, downscale histograms, and structural entropy. The text engine uses token frequency, set overlap, edit distance, and bigram order. Both run locally. There is no model file, no cloud inference, no GPU server. Read more.
Does VFYIELD upload my photos or text?
No. Nothing leaves your device. VFYIELD performs no network requests at all — no analytics, no crash reporting, no remote configuration. Read the full privacy policy.
Will VFYIELD work on iPad?
Yes. VFYIELD renders with a tabbed layout on iPhone and a sidebar layout on iPad. All features are identical on both form factors.
Will it work in Airplane Mode?
Yes. VFYIELD does not need internet access.
Can I delete my history?
Yes. Open Library → History and tap Clear. Deleting the app uninstalls everything.

Troubleshooting

The camera button doesn't open the camera

Open iOS Settings → VFYIELD → Camera and make sure it is set to Allow. If you previously selected Deny, iOS will not show the prompt again until you reset access in Settings.

"Choose from Library" opens but I cannot see all of my photos

This is expected. We use PHPickerViewController, which is a separate picker process. It does not require granting access to your entire library — you select one photo at a time and that selection is what we receive.

Result card won't save to Photos

Open iOS Settings → VFYIELD → Photos and switch from None / Selected Photos to Add Photos Only. Then re-trigger the Save to Photos flow inside the app.

An analysis was scored but doesn't match what I see

That can happen. The image engine is a heuristic — it scores structure, not meaning. Try a clearer, more centered subject (good lighting, single dominant color, no small text), or compare with the seven reference archetypes in the overview.

Haptics don't fire / my phone stays silent

Open Settings inside the app and toggle Haptic Feedback. Also make sure your iPhone is not in Silent Mode (the switch on the left side of your device).

The theme switcher doesn't change everything

Some system widgets (status bar tint, certain segmented controls) only re-render when you switch tabs or reopen the relevant screen. The accent always updates everywhere — just give the device a beat.
